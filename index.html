Course description

Are you wanting to live in a way that respects:

~ Your more spiritual side because you're tired of hiding who you really are?

~ The kinder and more receptive ways of treating yourself because the 'be in control and uber competent at so many things' mentality is wearing you out?

~ Knowing and allowing both your light and dark sides so that you can accept yourself more and be your whole self in your relationships?

This course is designed to step you thru 8 aspects of your S.O.U.L. Style. It will guide you in knowing what is yearning to be known, healed, and empowered in your life and relationships to others.

It will also help you connect with the deeper aspects of your work and how you help others.
